About Yellow Light ARTS

Where Music Meets Excellence

Welcome to Yellow Light Arts, a premier destination for unforgettable live performances. Located in the heart of Athens, our concert hall is more than just a venue—it’s a cultural landmark where music, artistry, and community come together.





Our Story

Founded in April 2011, Yellow Light Arts was built with a vision to create a space where music lovers of all kinds could experience the joy of live performances. Over the years, we’ve hosted world-class artists, rising stars, and inspiring community events, making us a cornerstone of the local arts scene.

Our Venue

Designed with impeccable acoustics and elegant architecture, Yellow Light Arts offers an unmatched experience for audiences and performers alike.

Our Mission

At Yellow Light ARTS, our mission is to inspire, connect, and uplift through the power of music. We’re dedicated to: showcasing diverse genres and talents, supporting local and international artists and creating memorable experiences for every guest.

Join the Experience

From timeless classics to contemporary hits, our events cater to every musical taste. Whether you’re here for a symphony, a rock concert, or a community celebration, we promise an experience that stays with you long after the final note.
